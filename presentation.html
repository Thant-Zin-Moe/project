RULES

  • A match consists of two 45 minutes and in between 15 minutes rest period
  • Each team has 11 players including 1 goalkeeper and a minimum of 7 players are needed to constitute
  • Each game must include one refree and two assistant refree's(linesmen)
  • For foul, committed player could receive either a yellow card or red card.
  • A yellow card is warning and a red card is a dismissal of that player.Two yellow card will equal one red card.Once a player is sent off then they cannot be replaced
  • If a ball goes out off an opponent in either of the side lines then it is given as a throw in.If it goes out of play off an attacking player on the base line then it is a goal kick.If it comes off a defending player it is a corner kick.
